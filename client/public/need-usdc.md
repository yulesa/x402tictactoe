# Getting USDC

For Base mainnet, you need real USDC. There are many ways of buying it, we suggest 2 below. For Base Sepolia testnet, you can get free test USDC by following the last steps.

## Option A - Integrated on-ramp: When the the Demo is running on Base Mainnet (Real Money)

1. **Click Buy in your wallet menu** — **MAKE SURE YOU'VE SELECTED THE RIGHT NETWORK AND TOKEN.** Minimum recommended amount is 2 USD. "Pay with card" also supports PIX.
2. **Select your Payment Provider and Confirm Payment** — You will be redirect to the payment processor page. We recommend Coinbase, they also support PIX. If this is your first time, you need to pass through verification since they offer exchange services (Know your Client is required in this step). 
3. **Register payment method** —  Coinbase allow credit card and PIX payments under "Register payment method". PIX deposits will turn into a BRL balance at Coinbase that later can be exchanged to USDC. Select your payment method. Confirm and buy.

## Option B - Peanut: When the the Demo is running on Base Mainnet (Real Money)

We suggest Peanut because they accept PIX, but bridging to browser wallet will be necessary.

1. **Sign up to Peanut.me** — Use your phone and head to [peanut.me](https://peanut.me). You can use the invite YULEINVITESYOU547.
2. **Add funds** — Click "Add" to add funds, if this is your first time, you need to pass through verification since they offer exchange services (Know your Client is required in this step). You can use PIX as payment method. Make sure to add at least 1 USD value.
3. **Bridge funds to your wallet** — Click "Withdraw" to allow sending the funds to another address. Select Crypto as method. Select BASE network and USDC token. Paste your tic-tac-toe wallet in the wallet address field. **MAKE SURE YOU'VE SELECTED THE RIGHT NETWORK AND TOKEN. BASE IS NOT DEFAULT**.
4. Review and confirm.

# Selling USDC to cash

1. **Send funds to your exchange** — Click Send in your wallet menu. **MAKE SURE YOU'VE SELECTED THE RIGHT NETWORK AND TOKEN.** You need to get the deposit or wallet address from the exchenge or Peanut. **MAKE SURE YOU'VE THE RIGHT ADDRESS, FUNDS SEND TO WRONG ADDRESSES ARE PERMANENTLY LOST.**
2. **Withdraw cash** — Follow the withdraw process in the exchange or Peanut.

# Getting USDC on Testnet

Possible when the Demo is running on Base Sepolia Testnet (Free)

1. Go to the CDP Faucet at [faucet.cdp.coinbase.com](https://portal.cdp.coinbase.com/products/faucet)
2. Select "Base Sepolia" network.
3. Select USDC token (not default).
4. Paste your wallet address
5. Claim USDC tokens

The faucet will send test USDC to your wallet. These tokens have no real value but work exactly like the real thing for testing purposes.
